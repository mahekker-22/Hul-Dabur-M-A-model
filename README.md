# M&A Accretion / Dilution Model: HUL & Dabur India

A comprehensive Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) buy-side transaction model simulating the strategic acquisition of **Dabur India (Target)** by **Hindustan Unilever (Acquirer)** within the Indian FMCG sector.

> **Disclaimer:** These models and reports were created for academic and professional portfolio development purposes. The analysis relies on historical financial disclosures and assumptions constructed solely to demonstrate financial modeling proficiency. They do not constitute formal investment, legal, or financial advice.

## 📌 Project Overview
This model assesses the structural financial impact of a large-scale consumer goods consolidation. It evaluates transaction metrics, financing structures, Purchase Price Allocation (PPA), brand amortization schedules, and pro-forma EPS impacts across multiple synergy targets.

## 🛠️ Key Model Mechanics & Core Features
* **Transaction Architecture:** Structured a ₹145,642 Cr Enterprise Value transaction utilizing a **50% Cash / 50% Stock** payment mix. 
* **Debt Financing:** Modeled the raising of ₹70,299 Cr in new corporate debt to fund the cash component of the offer.
* **Premium Scenarios:** Tested market reaction metrics based on acquisition premiums:
  * **Bear Scenario:** 25.0% Premium | Offer Price: ₹786.3
  * **Base Scenario:** 30.0% Premium | Offer Price: ₹817.7
  * **Bull Scenario:** 35.0% Premium | Offer Price: ₹849.2
* **Purchase Price Allocation (PPA):** Modeled the write-up of target assets, capturing ₹13,000 Cr in total Intangible Step-Ups:
  * *Brand Value:* ₹8,000 Cr (15-year useful life)
  * *Customer Relationships:* ₹3,500 Cr (10-year useful life)
  * *Technology/IP:* ₹1,500 Cr (10-year useful life)
* **Tax Accounting:** Factored a 25% **Deferred Tax Liability (DTL)** rate on asset step-ups (₹3,250 Cr) to calculate a final residual Goodwill of ₹124,462 Cr.
* **Amortization Waterfall:** Built a 3-year post-merger amortization schedule tracking non-cash expenses (₹1,033 Cr annually) flowing through the pro-forma income statement.
